# Product Context

## Purpose

Ball is Wine is a creative writing project that combines two passions:

1. **Basketball**: Watching and analyzing NBA and WNBA games
2. **Wine**: Exploring and documenting wine tasting experiences

The project serves as a unique intersection of these seemingly unrelated interests, creating a distinctive voice and perspective in both the sports and wine writing spaces.

## Target Audience

- Basketball fans who might be curious about wine
- Wine enthusiasts who might enjoy a different perspective on wine tasting
- Readers who appreciate creative, personal writing that connects different experiences
- People who enjoy seeing connections between disparate interests and activities

## User Experience Goals

### For Readers

- **Accessibility**: Content should be approachable for both basketball fans and wine enthusiasts, without requiring expertise in either area
- **Authenticity**: Writing should feel personal and genuine, reflecting real experiences
- **Discoverability**: Content should be easily browsable by date, wine type, or basketball themes
- **Visual Appeal**: Wine bottle images and clean typography enhance the reading experience

### For the Author

- **Consistency**: Monthly writing cadence provides structure
- **Creative Freedom**: Format allows for both structured data (wine details) and free-form writing
- **Documentation**: Creates a record of both basketball moments and wine experiences over time
- **Growth**: Opportunity to develop knowledge about wine while writing about a familiar subject (basketball)

## Problem Solving

This project solves several problems:

1. **Creative Outlet**: Provides a structured but flexible format for creative writing
2. **Knowledge Building**: Creates motivation to learn more about wine through regular tasting and documentation
3. **Memory Preservation**: Captures thoughts about basketball games and wine that would otherwise be forgotten
4. **Unique Perspective**: Offers a fresh take on both wine and basketball writing by connecting the two

## Content Strategy

- **Monthly Entries**: Each entry combines basketball and wine experiences from the same time period
- **Dual Focus**: Equal attention to both basketball analysis and wine tasting notes
- **Visual Elements**: Wine bottle images enhance the content
- **Structured Data**: Wine information (producer, vintage, grape) is captured in a consistent format
- **Personal Voice**: Writing maintains a consistent, authentic voice across both subjects
